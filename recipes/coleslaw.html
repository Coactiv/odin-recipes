Sweet Restaurant Slaw

A good all-around slaw.

This coleslaw is on the sweet side, which makes it pair nice with most savory dishes.

It's great on the side of some BBQ or inside a burger or sandwich. You're going to want to eat it with everything.

Ingredients

  • 16oz coleslaw mix
  • 2 tbsp diced onion
  • 2/3 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp poppy seeds
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine onion and coleslaw mix.
  2. In a seperate bowl whisk together the salada dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt. Then pour it over the coleslaw and coat it well.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
