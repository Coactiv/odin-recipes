Guacamole

The holy grail of dips.

This delicious mush of avacados will have you wanting to eat for every meal.

With cilantro and cayenne this recipe will a guarantee flavor in a every bite. Serve it as smooth, or chunky, as you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 3 avacados - peeled and pitted
  • 1 lime juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 3 tbsp chapped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Combine and mash all ingradients in a bowl.
  2. Serve and enjoy.
